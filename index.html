Discover The Great Taste of Coffee

Hive Cafe is a warm and welcoming coffee shop located in the hear of New Westminster. Our passion for specialty coffee and delicious, wholesome food is what sets us apart from the rest.

We offer a wide variety of coffee


Including espresso-based drinks, pour overs, and cold brew. Consider trying our single-origin and seasonal coffee options, as well as alternative milk options such as soy, almond and oat milk.

We also have baked goods that pair well with our drinks


Consider ordering some healthy options such as our yogurt parfait with granola and fruits. Or indulge in our dipped donuts. Made fresh every day.
